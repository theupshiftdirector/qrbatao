How to Choose the Right QR Code Generator

The best QR code generator depends on your specific needs. Here is a guide to help you decide:

  • Personal or one-time use — A free tool like QR Batao is all you need. Create static QR codes for URLs, WiFi passwords, contact cards, or UPI payments.
  • Small business — Start with free static QR codes for your shop, restaurant menu, or payment. Upgrade to dynamic QR codes when you need to update links without reprinting.
  • Marketing campaigns — Use QR Tiger or Beaconstac for dynamic QR codes with scan analytics, A/B testing, and retargeting pixels.
  • Enterprise — Beaconstac and Uniqode offer team management, SSO, white-labeling, and CRM integrations for large-scale deployments.
  • Developers — QR Tiger, Beaconstac, and Scanova provide REST APIs for programmatic QR code generation and management.

For most individuals and small businesses, free static QR codes work perfectly. Dynamic QR codes become essential when you need to change the destination URL after printing, track scan metrics, or manage QR codes at scale.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free QR code generator?

QR Batao is the best completely free QR code generator with no sign-up required. It supports URL, text, WiFi, UPI, and vCard QR codes with PNG and SVG downloads. QR Code Monkey also offers a generous free tier with logo customization. For basic static QR codes, free tools work perfectly.

What is the difference between static and dynamic QR codes?

A static QR code encodes data directly — once created, it cannot be changed. A dynamic QR code contains a short redirect URL that can be updated anytime without reprinting the QR code. Dynamic QR codes also enable scan tracking and analytics. Free tools typically offer static codes, while premium tools offer dynamic codes.

Do I need a paid QR code generator?

For personal use or small businesses creating static QR codes, free generators like QR Batao are sufficient. You need a paid generator if you require dynamic QR codes (editable after printing), scan analytics, bulk generation, branded short URLs, or API access for integration with your systems.

Which QR code generator is best for business?

For enterprise use, Beaconstac and Uniqode are top choices with advanced analytics, team management, and API access. QR Tiger offers the best value for mid-size businesses with dynamic QR codes starting at affordable plans. For small businesses, QR Batao (free) covers most basic needs.

Can QR codes expire?

Static QR codes never expire — they encode data directly and work forever. Dynamic QR codes depend on the generator's redirect service, so they may stop working if you cancel your subscription or the service shuts down. With free tools like QR Batao, static codes are generated in your browser and work permanently.
