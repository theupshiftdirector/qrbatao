Frequently Asked Questions

What is a QR code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that can store data like URLs, text, WiFi credentials, payment info, and contact details. It can be scanned using any smartphone camera.

Is QR Batao free to use?

Yes, QR Batao is completely free. You can generate unlimited QR codes with no signup, no watermarks, and instant PNG & SVG downloads.

Do the QR codes expire?

No. QR codes generated by QR Batao are static codes — they encode data directly and never expire. They will work as long as the encoded data (like a URL) is valid.

What QR code types can I generate?

You can generate QR codes for URLs (website links), plain text, WiFi network credentials, UPI payment links (Indian payment apps), and vCard contact cards.

Can I download QR codes in SVG format?

Yes. QR Batao supports both PNG and SVG downloads. SVG is vector-based, so it scales perfectly for print materials like business cards, posters, and banners.

How does the UPI QR code work?

The UPI QR code encodes your UPI ID, payee name, and optionally an amount and note. When scanned with any UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), it pre-fills the payment details for the sender.
